Our Mission

Welcome to the All-In-One Functional Calculator project. Our goal was to create a simple, fast, and completely free online tool that serves your everyday calculation needs without any clutter. We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality, easy-to-use utilities on any device, whether you're on a desktop computer or a mobile phone.

What We Built

This website is a single-page application that houses four powerful tools:

  • A scientific calculator with standard and advanced functions.
  • A geometry calculator for finding the area and volume of various 2D and 3D shapes.
  • A handy unit converter for length, weight, and temperature.
  • A simple date & time calculator to quickly determine age.

This project was built using standard web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure it is fast, reliable, and accessible to everyone. We hope you find it useful!
